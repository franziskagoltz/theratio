THE:RATIO has a mission to change the ratio of men and women in tech by providing transparency over company ratios and statistics.

We believe that this transparency will push companies to work harder to create women-friendly environments and ultimately improve their score.

Each tech company receives a rating score based on the following formula:

0.5(% women in tech departments) + 0.2(% all women employees) + 0.2(% women in leadership) + 0.1(employee survey avg)

In our view, companies with higher scores are more women-friendly.
